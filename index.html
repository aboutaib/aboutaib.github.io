I am a Ph.D. student working on Evolutionary Algorithms for Combinatorial Optimization. I prepare a double joint Ph.D. thesis at Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale , France, and Mohammed V University in Rabat , Faculty of Science , Morocco.
My thesis subject is about understanding the Evolutionary Algorithms Control Problem (EACP), especially in the distributed context for combinatorial optimization, and trying to solve it! I also work on understanding the fitness landscape of stochastic search operators.

  • Thesis supervisors: Sébastien Verel (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale) and Belaïd Ahiod (Mohammed V University in Rabat, Faculty of Science).
  • Thesis advisors: Bilel Derbel (Université de Lille, CRIStAL) and Cyril Fonlupt (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale).
