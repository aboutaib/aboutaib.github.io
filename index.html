Brahim Aboutaib

I am a PhD candidate in the intresection of Distributed Computing and Evolutionary Algorithms, with application to optimization problems. My PhD is a joint supervision from Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale, France, and Mohammed V University in Rabat, by Sébastien Verel and Belaïd Ahiod.

I am interested in simple models of natural evolution and their simulation on distributed environment. Practically, I work on understanding adaptation in evolutionary algorithms, and its potential of evolving (or not) solutions to hard combinatorial problems.
