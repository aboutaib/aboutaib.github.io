Brahim Aboutaib

Hello! I am a Ph.D. student working on evolutionary computation for combinatorial optimization. I prepare a double joint Ph.D. thesis at the Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale, France, and the Mohammed V University in Rabat, Faculty of Science, Morocco.

My thesis is about understanding the Evolutionary Algorithms Control Problem (EACP), especially in parallel and distributed context for combinatorial optimization, and trying to solve it. I also work on fitness landscape analysis.

  • Thesis supervisors: Sébastien Verel (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale) and Belaïd Ahiod (Mohammed V University in Rabat, Faculty of Science).
  • Thesis advisors: Bilel Derbel (Université de Lille, CRIStAL) and Cyril Fonlupt (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale).
