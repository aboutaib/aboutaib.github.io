Brahim Aboutaib

Hi !

I am a Ph.D. student working on evolutionary computation for combinatorial optimization. I prepare a double joint Ph.D. thesis at Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale , France, and Mohammed V University in Rabat , Faculty of Science , Morocco.

My thesis is about understanding the Evolutionary Algorithms Control Problem (EACP), especially in parallel and distributed context for combinatorial optimization, and trying to solve it. I also work on fitness landscape analysis of stochastic optimization algorithms.

  • Thesis supervisors: Sébastien Verel (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale) and Belaïd Ahiod (Mohammed V University in Rabat, Faculty of Science).
  • Thesis advisors: Bilel Derbel (Université de Lille, CRIStAL) and Cyril Fonlupt (Université du Littoral Côte d'Opale).
